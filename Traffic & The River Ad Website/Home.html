Traffic & The River Official Website v1.0, by Gregory Desrosiers

Welcome to the official website of Traffic & The River, a music video pack where two videos will take you in a direction that you would ought to consider: Can we hold off Mother Nature as we try to respect ourselves in terms of freedom through  technology?

This may be a question that involves so much thinking, but if you watch the videos, you should be able to earn a sensation of what it’s like to live with freedom, but at an environmental level. Of course, there are pros and cons to every move you make, but if you stick with the right direction, you should be able to feel like you’re an ambassador of environment, meaning you know so much about the environment that you take action to offer respect to the environment yet you still have much freedom.

Traffic & The River combines music with the visuals into an effective film that still matches the quality of conventional relaxation or techno videos.  Whether you watch Planet Earth by BBC or Freddie Wong’s Arcade Dominator, the whole idea of fun is presentable as soon as you watch the first minute of the film. It’s the same thing with Traffic & The River; even with a limited amount of enhancing techniques, the reliability of visuals and the music following the subject of both videos presents a strong, melodramatic world that you would eventually become part of the environment and just do nothing except enjoy the scenery.
